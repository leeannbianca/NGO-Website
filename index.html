About Us

Banareng e-Theatre is a center that assists in promoting Artistic and Cultural activities such as drama, poetry, dance and many other more.
With these activities the company would like to honor our norms, ethics and values as an African child


MISSION

To out-root talent within our children for them to realize their potential and identity as an African child To assist in the eradication of crime from our environment and the society; thus promoting a crime-free zone To promote the Spirit of Ubuntu and togetherness in our society





VISION

To retain the dignity of an African child and the entire society by honoring our values, ethics and cultural standards (roots); thus building a unique human being who will diligently and passionately display these aspects of life through performances locally and globally with pride





OUR BELIEF

We believe we can restore a sense of respect and love within our youth and the entire community through Art and Cultural education as we believe that it takes a village to raise a child

Programs

We offer the following programs



Talent Search Sessions

Developing the art of acting, music, poetry
and drawing during these sessions



Indigenous Games

Building a healthy lifestyle through a variety
of dance movements and indigenous games



African Pageants

Promoting the aesthetic (beauty) aspect of mankind
through pageants and demonstration of cultural wear



Social Prevention Education

The center offers social to primary prevention education programs to the youth,
to help them stay away from activities within our environment,
e.g. sexual abuse that may lead to being infected by HIV/AIDS,Streets dominated by bullies,drugs lots,gangsters and many other social ills
